Slow Speech



________________________________

Slow speech is one if not the most common strategies used to combat stuttering. As in the name slow speech is a strategy, that requires you to decrease your speech rate, and by doing so it makes a big impact! When you speak slowly the amount of stress built up in your vocal cords decreases. Stress is one of the main factors to stuttering, so by reducing it you can become much more fluent.



________________________________

You can use slow speech during a time when you feel like you're going to stutter. For example, if you're about to read a paragraph out loud, and feel like you're going to stutter it's useful to use slow speech to reduce your stress, and increase your fluency.



________________________________

Here are some sentences to practice slow speech (rember that during these sentences to speak at a slow rate, to where your fluency increases.)

1. The dog ate a bunch of bones.

2. The man looked out at the beach.

3. In life you should try to maintain a healthy diet.

4. Stop eating the cookies! Cried my mother.

5. I think that Soccer is the best sport. Said Jimmy.

6. Skateboarding is a lot of fun when you learn how to do it.

7. I it when it snows, because I can make snowmen!

8. The class laughed when the student made the joke.

9. I want the red ruler! Cried Randy.

10. Tommy constantly found himself in trouble.
