Slow Speech

Slow speech is the foundational technique speech coaches use to help smooth out a stutter. As in the name suggests, slow speech takes your reading and vocalization down to a turtle crawl. This releases tension in your vocal chords and helps you focus on the isolated word. The slow speech technique yields a big impact, and itâ€™s a great one to get you started. And, as we all know, the turtle wins the race!
