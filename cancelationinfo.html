Cancelation

________________________________

Cancelation along with slow speech is an important and commonly used strategy for stuttering. Cancelation like slow speech is meant to relieve some stress, to speak more fluently.

________________________________

Cancelation is used during a stutter. When you are in a stutter, you need to stop and take a relaxed, non-rushed, breath, and then proceed to speak. By taking that quick breath you're relieving stress therefore making you more fluent in your speaking.
