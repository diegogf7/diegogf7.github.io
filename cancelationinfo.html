Cancelation



________________________________

Cancelation alike with slow speech is considered one of the most important strategies to combat stuttering. This excercise like slow speech is commonly taught to beginners. While it may seem complicated at first, don't worry! Cancelation after a bit of practicing becomes a breeze to learn.



________________________________

Unlike slow speech you use cancelation during a stutter. When you're stuck in a stutter such as a block or repetition, you need to stop, take and deep yet slow breath, which gets rid of some stress, and try to say the word again.



Here are some sentences to practice cancelation, (Do a fake stutter on the highlighted word, and after a feel seconds practice stopping and taking a breath, and viola!)



1. The dog ate a bunch of bones.

2. The man looked out at the beach.

3. In life you should try to maintain a healthy diet.

4. Stop eating the cookies! Cried my mother.

5. I think that Soccer is the best sport. Said Jimmy.

6. Skateboarding is a lot of fun when you learn how to do it.

7. I it when it snows, because I can make snowmen!

8. The class laughed when the student made the joke.

9. I want the red ruler! Cried Randy.

10. Tommy constantly found himself in trouble.
