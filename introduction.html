Who is Stammr?

Stammr is a community service project created by Diego Gonzalez to help people with a stutter. Our mission is to open source the most important techniques to help and coach you through this. While only YOU can practice and set your mind to work through a stutter, Stammr arms you with tried and true techniques gathered from coaches and research to make your practice more effective.

From Diego: Hi, I’m Diego Gonzalez. I’m 13 years old and am building Stammr to help people who, like me, have a stutter. I started stuttering in the 7th grade, and my stutter got worse and worse until I could barely get a few words. Through coaching and tons of practice, I’ve been able to overcome and manage my stutter, and I wanted to create this website to help others who maybe don’t have access to a stuttering coach or want to practice on their own at home. I hope that this website helps you tackle your stutter head-on and keep practicing.
