Pausing and Chunking



________________________________

Pausing and chunking is one of the more advanced strategies for combating stuttering. Like slow speech its used throughout your time speaking. While, pausing and chunking might be complicated for you at first, don't worry with practice you'll learn how to master it.



________________________________

When you begin speaking you want to say about 3-5 words, and then take a brief pause not a comma long, but relatively shorter, where you get to plan ahead, for a bit and take a brief break.
